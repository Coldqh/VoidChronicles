# Deep Discovery v0.2

## Information ladder

1. Passive galaxy knowledge reveals a system.
2. System scan reveals planetary orbits and basic data.
3. Detailed scan materializes deterministic points of interest.
4. Field expeditions create evidence and may resolve a point.

## Causal POI generation

A point of interest is derived from the galaxy seed, system, planet, nearby civilizations and planetary environment. Its truth, public summary, equipment requirements and rewards remain stable across reloads.

## Evidence and hypotheses

Field objects yield evidence with kind and reliability. Evidence for the same point is combined into one hypothesis. Confidence rises through quantity, reliability and independent evidence types.

## Return visits

A POI can remain blocked when the player lacks required equipment. Its visit count changes the local surface seed while the historical identity remains stable.
