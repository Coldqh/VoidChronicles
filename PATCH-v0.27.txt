Void Chronicles v0.27.0 — Chronicle, World Gameplay & Consequences

Combined release for roadmap versions v0.25, v0.26 and v0.27.

New files:
- src/simulation/chronicle.ts
- src/simulation/worldGameplay.ts
- src/simulation/playerConsequences.ts
- src/tests/chronicleGameplayConsequences.test.ts

Changed files:
- src/simulation/projections.ts
- src/screens/LegacyScreen.tsx
- src/screens/WorldScreen.tsx
- src/version.ts
- README.md

v0.25 Chronicle 2.0:
- merges Deep Time and live events into one readable history;
- filters by date, domain, location, civilization, faction, entity and player involvement;
- traverses causal chains in both directions;
- compares two historical periods;
- excludes hidden event-sourced snapshots from the readable Chronicle.

v0.26 World-generated gameplay:
- derives concrete needs from shortages, migration, war, ecology, disasters, politics, research, discovery and heritage crises;
- converts those needs into compatible contracts with explicit success and failure consequences;
- exposes current requests in Living World and Chronicle interfaces.

v0.27 Player consequences:
- applies completed, failed and expired world-generated contracts to real simulation state;
- changes settlements, population, routes, systems, ecosystems, heritage, wars and factions;
- records every intervention as a causal player-world-consequence event;
- reconciliation is idempotent and cannot apply one contract result twice.

Compatibility:
- SAVE_SCHEMA_VERSION remains 13;
- no persistence migration is required;
- includes the v0.24.1 era-transition/regression priority fix;

Validation performed:
- strict TypeScript validation of simulation modules;
- strict JSX/type validation of Chronicle and Living World screens;
- living-history transition and regression priority regression check;
- 26 deterministic tests covering causal history, states, wars, economy, culture, society, figures, institutions, heritage, planets, Chronicle, generated gameplay and player consequences;
- ZIP structure, overlay and SHA-256 verification.

Run `npm run check` after applying the patch to validate the complete repository and production build.
