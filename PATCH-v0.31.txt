Void Chronicles v0.31.0 — Living Contacts & Interface Rework

Numeric integrity
- Ecology metrics are normalized to integers after direct changes, civilization pressure and save loading.
- Old saves containing values such as 70.68999999999998 are repaired automatically.
- Shared formatters were added for integers, percentages, metrics and population.

Interface rework
- The Civilizations navigation entry is now Contacts.
- A new responsive contact dossier replaces the old encyclopedia-first screen.
- Global panel, navigation, spacing and mobile presentation received a modern visual pass.
- Knowledge remains progressive: unknown political, cultural and access fields stay hidden.

Living diplomacy
- Trade proposals affect supply, prosperity and settlement stocks.
- Aid affects food, water, medicine, health and unrest.
- Landing requests unlock territory and settlement intelligence.
- Scientific exchange affects research and knowledge.
- Heritage can be returned directly to its creators.
- Trusted contacts can receive mediation proposals during active crises.
- Outcomes depend on culture, outsider policy, trust, respect, suspicion and a diplomat specialist.
- Agreements and diplomatic history are reconstructed from causal world events and survive saves without a schema migration.

Installation
1. Extract this archive over C:\VoidChronicles.
2. Run: node APPLY-v0.31.mjs
3. Run: npm run check
