Void Chronicles v0.29.0 — Progressive World Intelligence

Base: v0.28.1 or newer.
Save schema: 13, no migration.

Changes:
- adds a five-level intelligence model: unknown, rumor, observed, confirmed, verified;
- derives access from knowledge records, source confidence, contact stage and data age;
- removes the player-facing "entire simulation" Chronicle mode;
- hides Deep Time records until systems, civilizations or archives are known;
- redacts names, exact values, participants and causal links at low intelligence levels;
- adds knowledge-safe Chronicle comparison and causal tracing;
- replaces omniscient polity, war and settlement cards with progressive dossiers;
- gates economy, society, figures, institutions, heritage and planetary pressure behind suitable sources;
- adds deterministic tests for secrecy, rumors, contact progression, staleness and known-only comparisons.

Validation performed in the patch harness:
- strict TypeScript for simulation modules;
- strict JSX/type validation for WorldScreen and LegacyScreen;
- 44 deterministic tests across v0.18-v0.29 systems.
